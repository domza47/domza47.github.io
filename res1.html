Resources in Croatia

Poliklinika za zaštitu djece i mladih Grada Zagreba (Child and Youth Protection Center of Zagreb): a clinic that provides psychological support to children and families affected by bullying and other forms of trauma. They also conduct research and promote awareness campaigns.

Hrabri Telefon (Brave Phone): a hotline and counseling service for children and families dealing with abuse and bullying. They provide guidance on handling bullying situations and offer online counseling.

Web site made during the Erasmus+ project Breaking the silence in Ponte de Lima.
